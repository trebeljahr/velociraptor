Credits & Asset Sources

This game is a clone of the Chrome "dino" offline game, re-skinned with dinosaur sprites and assets. Credits:

Music

  • Background music — "L'Etoile danse (Pt. 1)" by Meydän, from the album Havor (2018), licensed under CC BY 4.0 via Free Music Archive.

Sound Effects

  • Rain ambience — sound effect by Pig Bank - Mood from Pixabay.
  • Thunder clap — sound effect by soundmarker33 from Pixabay.
  • Cactus collision (marimba lose) — sound effect by Universfield from Pixabay.
  • UFO hover — sound effect by SoundReality from Pixabay.
  • Santa sleigh bells — sound effect by DRAGON-STUDIO from Pixabay.

Images

  • cactus1.png – cactus8.png — eight cactus illustrations extracted from "Big and small cactuses vector illustrations set", designed by Freepik . Used under the Freepik Free License (attribution required).
  • raptor.gif — pixel-art dinosaur animation by Chris Masna ("Run Forrest Run" on DeviantArt). Used with explicit permission from Chris Masna.
  • party-hat.png — party hat illustration from "Party hat icon isolated design" on Freepik. Used under the Freepik Free License (attribution required). Background removed locally for in-game compositing.
  • bow-tie.png — red bow tie illustration from "Black white red bow tie set" on Freepik. Used under the Freepik Free License (attribution required). Cropped and background removed locally for in-game compositing.
  • thug-glasses.png — the classic pixel-art "thug life" sunglasses from File:Thug_Life_Glasses.png on Wikimedia Commons, by user Aboulharakat (2016). Licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0. Background flood-filled to transparent locally so the highlights on the lenses survive.

Engine & code

  • Vanilla HTML5 canvas, requestAnimationFrame, and the native <audio> element — no external game engine or library.
